Welcome to the Burnley Arts Festival website! This is our main spot for sharing everything that’s happening around the festival and all the cool things we’re doing to bring people in Burnley together. We set up this site to show off the talent, creativity, and good vibes that make our town unique. Here, you can check out updates about upcoming events, projects we’re working on, and different ways you can get involved—whether you’re into music, art, performances, or just want something fun to do.

We really want this festival to feel like something everyone can be part of, no matter how old you are or what you’re interested in. It’s all about celebrating the community, supporting local artists, and giving people a chance to try new things or show off what they can do. So take your time, explore the site, see what’s coming up, and get excited about being involved in something that brings Burnley together and highlights the people who make it such a great place to live.



  • We host many events throughout the year, hosting 26 events over the year of 2024
  • We are aim to host the most events ever this year, aiming for over 30 events over the course of 2025
  • Our events are hosted monthly, and are located all around Burnley, so watch out for us in your area.
  • Our events allow the whole commmunity of burnley to meet and connect and show of the creativity of Burnley to its community.
  • All our event staff are volunteers who want to make every event a great event for the community.
