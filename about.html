Our festivals have been running since 2010 and have gathered hundreds of people together to experience some of the best events of their life. Our staff are kind people who volunteer their time to put amazing shows on to the Burnley public, which are guaranteed to be great. We have many attractions at all our events, with some including stalls, main stage, carnival rides and many more places to boost your experience at our events.

  • We are one of the only festivals in the world that offers free entry!
  • The record crowd to one of our events was over 250 people in December 2015
  • The most events we have hosted in year is 20, which was this year!
  • Our longest event we have ever hosted is 8 hours - which over 50% of the crowed stayed the whole event!
  • Since starting we have had over 2000 guests come and view our events
  • Around 300 children took part in the workshops at our last christmas event - one of our records!
  • Our top turn out for volunteers was 100 for one event!
  • Over 15 different artistss came to our last event for towns and cities around the Burnley area!
  • Our main stage has hosted over 40 diffrent guests this year!
  • Our festivals have different stalls and wworkshops, so there is somthing for everyone!

We’re always trying new ideas to make our events more fun every year. From cool workshops to local performers, there’s always something different to enjoy. What matters most to us is bringing people together and creating a friendly, welcoming atmosphere where everyone can have a good time.
